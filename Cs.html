Cybersecurity Awareness Website

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks and data from digital attacks, hacking and unauthorized access.

Common Cyber Threats

Phishing
Malware
Ransomware
Social Engineering
SQL Injection
DDoS Attacks

Why Cybersecurity is Important?

Without security, personal information or data can be stolen or misused. Cybersecurity prevents online attacks and protects privacy.

Basic Safety Tips

  • Use strong passwords
  • Do not click unknown links
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Update software regularly
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking
